# 🥗 Comprehensive Analysis and Dietary Strategies with Tableau

An interactive Tableau dashboard that analyzes students' dietary habits, lifestyle choices, health indicators, and academic performance. This project transforms raw survey data into meaningful visual insights to help understand relationships between nutrition, exercise, and overall well-being.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project explores various factors that influence students' health and dietary behaviors, including:

- 🍽️ Breakfast preferences
- 🍳 Cooking vs Eating Out
- 🏃 Exercise habits
- ⚖️ Weight distribution
- ❤️ Health category
- 😊 Life satisfaction
- 🍫 Comfort food reasons
- 🎓 Academic performance (GPA)

The dashboard enables users to interactively explore trends and patterns for better decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze students' eating habits and health status.
- Identify dietary patterns and lifestyle behaviors.
- Compare health metrics across gender.
- Understand emotional reasons behind comfort food consumption.
- Build an interactive dashboard using Tableau.

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 🍽️ Gender vs Breakfast
- Compares breakfast preferences.
- Shows cereal is the most preferred breakfast option.

### 🍳 Eating Out vs Cooking
- Visualizes eating-out frequency versus cooking habits.
- Gender-wise comparison.

### ❤️ Health Category by Gender
- Compares healthy and unhealthy students.
- Displays gender-based health distribution.

### 😊 People Feeling Healthy and Life Rewarded
- Bubble chart representing health satisfaction.
- Visualizes overall well-being responses.

### ⚖️ Weight vs Exercise
- Treemap showing weight distribution.
- Helps identify exercise-related patterns.

### 🍫 Comfort Food Reasons
- Bubble chart showing emotional eating triggers.
- Major reasons include:
  - Stress
  - Boredom
  - Sadness
